# 🏆 FanVerse AI | Judge Demo Script (Agentic Focus)

Welcome to the future of fan engagement. **FanVerse AI** is not just a dashboard; it's a living ecosystem orchestrated by multiple specialized AI agents.

## 🤖 The Agentic Core
The platform runs on a **Multi-Agent Orchestrator** that coordinates 8+ specialized agents:

1.  **NarrativeAgent**: Crafts an emotional "Director's Cut" of the match in real-time.
2.  **StrategicAgent**: Analyzes tactical shifts (e.g., bowling changes) and provides expert insights.
3.  **PredictionAgent**: Manages the real-time economy and fan sentiment wagering.
4.  **SocialAgent**: Monitors global fan energy and triggers "Viral Spikes."
5.  **EngagementAgent**: Dynamically launches polls and trivia when it detects "lulls" in the match flow.
6.  **DirectorAgent**: Manages the UI state, triggering "Cinematic Transitions" for major events.
